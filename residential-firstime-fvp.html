First-Time Shipper?

Whether you’re shipping a refrigerator to your new home, furniture to a relative or a television you just bought online, FreightCenter can save you time and money and help ship your items easily and safely. But first things first – what are you shipping? Is it freight or parcel? And how can you tell?

Freight vs. Parcel…

  • Over 150 poounds is freight, under is parcel
  • More than 108 inches in length is freight, under is parcel
  • More than 165 inches in both length and girth combined is freight, below is parcel.
  • Standard services from USPS, UPS and FedEx are used for parcel, not freight. This is where we come in – we can help with both parcel & freight shipping.
